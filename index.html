About Me

Hi! I’m Daisha—Georgia Tech grad 🐝 with a degree in Computer Science, former QA engineer, and an aspiring Product Manager with a heart for people and process.

My career started in quality assurance, where I strengthened my instincts for usability, quality, and collaborative problem-solving. Now I’m shifting into product management, because I want to shape what gets built, not just test it. I thrive where human-centered design meets technical execution, and I believe in inclusive, thoughtful products that are built with purpose and intention.

Problem solving is my happy place—whether I’m untangling a workflow, optimizing a feature, or figuring out how to beat the next level of a puzzle-heavy video game 🎮🧠. I'm passionate about designing for equity and celebrating perspectives that often go unheard. To me, inclusion is that extra step that I believe should never get skipped.

When I’m not solving puzzles behind the screen, I’m solving them in games 🎮, planning my next food-fueled trip ✈️🍜, or chasing giggles with my daughter and pup 👧🏽🐾

I bring curiosity, strategic thinking, and strong communication skills to every room I walk into—, plus a deep respect for engineering, collaboration, and the humans we’re building for.
